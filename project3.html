Capri, Itay!

This beautiful, upscale island is the perfect place for your next vacation. Capri has hotels, shopping, designer fashions, limoncello and handmade leather sandals. One of its best-known natural sites is the Blue Grotto, a dark cavern where the sea glows electric blue, the result of sunlight passing through an underwater cave. This island is also home to many yachts and boat houses.
